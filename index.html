About Me

My name is Scott House. I am 31 years old and from Sanford, ME. I have Bachelor's Degrees in Political Science and History from the University of Michigan. For the past 6 years, I have worked in education, primarily teaching Title I math and literacy to elementary students. Last January, I went back to school at the University of Maine to pursue Computer Engineering and Computer Science degrees, but was forced to leave the program, due to a family emergency. I enjoy joshi puroresu, the occasional JRPG video game, and my Maine Coon cat, Henrik Catterberg.
